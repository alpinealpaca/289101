About us

Wyaaaaaa. I'm Chalmun. I've been the owner of this fine establishment for over 30 years now. Since taking over from the Vriichon brothers, we have maintained the spirit and integrity of this building. Long standing patrons have nothing to fear as they will have the experiences they have always loved. New customers are always welcome as long as they leave their droids at the door. (Droids brought inside will be dismantled forcefully by our barkeep.) As long as the house rules are followed, come and enjoy the strongest drinks on Tatooine, the best live music and our colourful patronage.
