Welcome to the Mos Eisley Cantina

Live Music!

Here at Chalmun's Cantina, we have live music every night by Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes. You'll find no finer cantina music in the Outer Rim.

Strong Drinks!

We serve some of the strongest and most popular drinks on Tatooine! Enjoy your favourites, like blue milk, Tatooine Sunset, Jawa Juice, Yatooni Boska, Tatooni Junko, and Hutt's Delight.

No Droids!

We pride ourselves on being able to create a droid-free environment for our guests. Focus on what really matters without those pesky droids!

Lively Hub!

Conveniently located in the heart of Mos Eisley, there's never a dull moment. We serve all sorts here, so it's never hard to locate any pilots, bounty hunters or any other sort of brokers and dealers you might be looking for.
